About Us


              Welcome to the largest watch collection platform WRISTY

Welcome to WRISTY! At WRISTY, we believe that a watch is more than just a timepiece. It is a statement of style, a reflection of personality, and a symbol of craftsmanship. Our mission is to provide you with a curated selection of high-quality watches that blend timeless elegance with modern innovation.

Our Story Founded in 2003, WRISTY began with a passion for horology and a vision to make luxury and sophistication accessible to all. Our founders, Sakshi Nikam and Pranali Bhor, have been enthusiasts of fine watches for decades, and their dedication to the art of watchmaking inspired the creation of this platform.

Join Our Community We invite you to join our community of watch enthusiasts. Follow us on social media for the latest updates, exclusive offers, and insights into the world of horology. Share your own watch stories and connect with fellow aficionados who share your passion. Thank you for choosing WRISTY. We look forward to being a part of your journey in discovering the perfect timepiece.
